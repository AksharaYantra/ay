Y-VitalSigns - Non-Invasive Medical App

This App has features to help you check your vital signs such as heartrate, cardiac function, blood sugars and detects the stage of your diabetes, blood pressure, respiratory rate, lung capacity, liver disorders and temperature. This App stores the data, updates your condition, and can send the data to your physician if you choose to do so. For more details, please visit www.Y-VitalSigns.com website.
